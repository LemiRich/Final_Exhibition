And then, maybe I was too greedy, maybe I was pushing myself too hard, and suddenly it hit me, and I really thought I was facing the worst time of my life. Even though I'm on my way back to health now, I realized something new during this time. Maybe it's actually a good thing that I was able to shut down during this time, when the world was collectively slowing down because of the pandemic, because it made me feel less deprived, because it wasn't just me, it was everyone else. And then when it became almost impossible to go about your daily life... I was so proud of myself for accomplishing even the smallest of tasks. Why didn't I console myself and praise myself more? Also, having spent a lot of time away from my family since high school due to dormitory life, I was so grateful and cherished the time I spent with them while battling my illness.
